Overview

Purpose

The Montevideo Chamber of Commerce helps foster and advocate for the community. Promoting business through services for residents and tourists, seeking community economic development, being a leader in defending the general interest of commerce, services, and the entire private business sector, as a driver of the economy.

Scenarios

  • Can the Chamber of Commerce of Uruguay facilitate our establishment process in the country?
  • Do they provide information about the local business environment, regulations, and how to establish contacts with reliable business partners and suppliers in Uruguay?
  • Does the Chamber of Commerce offer advisory services for entrepreneurs?
  • Where can I find information about legal requirements, taxes, and financing for startups in Uruguay?
  • How can the Chamber of Commerce of Uruguay help me identify export opportunities, provide information about international trade regulations, and connect with potential business partners abroad?
